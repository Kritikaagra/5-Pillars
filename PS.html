PROBLEM SOLVING

● Identify the real problem – not the symptom.
● Use the 5-Why model.
● Real Problems have an Implementable Solution and make a Real Difference And you can be a part of the solution!

Reverse Brainstorming

● Identify the problem.
● Reverse the problem
● Collect ideas
● Reverse the ideas.
● Evaluate the ideas and identify the solution.

Own the problem:

● Trap the self-imposed powerlessness and victimization.
● you disempower yourself
● The reverse of it is owning the problem and empowerment.
● Leaders empower themselves.
● Leader own the problem.

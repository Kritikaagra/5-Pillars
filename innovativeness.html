INNOVATIVENESS

> What is Innovativeness?

Creating a new product, service or process.

OR

Making an existing product or process more beneficial.

> Overcome your "chalta hai" attitude and do the following:

Think out of the box.

Find your time or space for creative thinking.

Overcoming assumptive limitation and associative barriers.

> How to overcome assumptive limitation and associative barriers?

Overcome the fear of being worng.

Ask rather than assume.

Reverse assumptions.

TAKING INITIATIVE

> What is the relationship between leadership and taking initiative?

> What should we consider before we take calulated risk?

1. Be as unemotional as possible.

2. Be truthful, use real data.

3. Be realistic.

4. Mitigate risk where possible.

5. Do I need to make this decision today?

> How do we overcome our fear of failure?

1. Reframe your goal.

2. Uncover your story.

3. Visualize obstacles and make plans for overcoming them.

4. Ask three questions:


a) What did I learn from this situation.
b) How can I grow from this experience.
c) What are three positive things about this situation.

> What do we need to consider to practise risk mitigation?

1. AVOID
2. ACCEPT
3. TRANSFER
4.LIMIT

CONSCIENTIOUSNESS

> CONSCIENTIOUSNESS is:

Doing the write thing even when no one is looking.

Conscientiousness was rated the most critical factor (88%) for competitiveness by influential survey respondents, and also the most scarce (19%) among Indian professionals. Building up your conscientiousness will therefore yield large dividends.

> Being Conscientious

To be Conscientious, you must:
● Know what is right- so you can do the right thing!
● Address internal conflicts about what to do.
● Develop the will to do the right thing.

> Personal code of conduct rules:

● Moral/Ethical
● Non-Trivial (stretch)
● Widely Applicable
● Enduring
● Practicable

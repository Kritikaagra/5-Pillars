PERSEVERANCE

> Why must individuals persevere in order to be competitive?

● Without perseverance, you can be left behind by people less talented and privileged than you are.
● The three ordinary things that we often don't pay enough attention to, but which I believe are the drivers of all success, are hard work, perseverance, and basic honesty.

To persevere:

● Identify your top-level goal
● Make a Pyramid of Goals

Strategies for staying focused on a Top Level goal

● Write down it and share it widely.
● Break it down into short-term tasks
● Reward yourself on completing a milestone.
● Persist.
● Give up activites/interest that come in the way.
